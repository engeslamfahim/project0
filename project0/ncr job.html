Senior Customer Service Engineer

Job description

  • Provide installation, maintenance and repairs on equipment within an assigned territory/region to assure continuity of customer operations and high levels of customer satisfaction.
  • Responsible for assessing customer problems or incidents until the situation has been resolved to the customer's satisfaction and are in compliance with agreed upon Service Level Agreements.
  • Provides primary service, including hardware maintenance, installation, network management, multi-vendor maintenance and software support.
  • Specializes in ATM repairs, router and network support, T.C.R (total cash recycler), DVD kiosks, Customer service .
